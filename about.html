About Madaraka Express Kenya

Madaraka Express Kenya is a premier high-speed railway service connecting Kenya's major cities. With state-of-the-art trains, Madaraka Express ensures that passengers travel safely, comfortably, and efficiently. Launched to provide a modern transportation alternative, we are committed to delivering top-notch customer service, reliability, and affordability to all Kenyans. From business travelers to families seeking adventure, Madaraka Express offers a unique way to explore Kenya's diverse landscapes and vibrant cities.
